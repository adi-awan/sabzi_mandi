About Sabzi Mandi

Our Story

Sabzi Mandi was founded with a simple mission: to deliver the freshest vegetables straight from farms to your kitchen. We partner directly with local farmers across Pakistan to bring you quality produce at fair prices.

Our team carefully selects and inspects every vegetable before it reaches your doorstep. We believe in supporting local agriculture while making fresh, healthy food accessible to every household.
